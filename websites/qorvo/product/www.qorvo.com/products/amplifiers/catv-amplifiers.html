    CATV Amplifiers

    Qorvo's amplifiers for cable TV (CATV) include single-ended, dual and differential amplifiers for a wide range of 75-ohm applications. These devices offer the versatility and economy needed to support triple-play requirements, system upgrades and greenfield deployments.

    Key Features

    • Single-ended and differential configurations
    • Best-in-class high linearity
    • Low power consumption
    • 3 V to 12 V

    Typical Applications

    • Cable TV
    • Fiber to the home (FTTH)
    • Satellite TV (SATV)
