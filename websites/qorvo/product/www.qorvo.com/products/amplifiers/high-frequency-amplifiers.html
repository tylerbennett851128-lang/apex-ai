    Products Amplifiers

    High Frequency Amplifiers (> 6 GHz)

    Qorvo's portfolio of amplifiers that operate over 6 GHz includes both GaAs and GaN devices, available in die and package forms.

    Key Features

    • Industry-leading power amplifiers using both GaAs and GaN technologies
    • Application-specific product focus (such as power or linearity)
    • Variety of power levels along with high PAE and gain performance
    • Small form factor

    Typical Applications

    • Military and commercial radar
    • Electronic warfare
    • Military and commercial satellite communications, including VSAT
    • Point-to-point radio
