    Products Amplifiers

    Gain Block Amplifiers

    Qorvo designs and manufactures a wide selection of general-purpose, internally matched MMIC amplifiers that are targeted for many applications in network infrastructure and defense / aerospace. These components showcase the wide range of performance capabilities using Qorvo's processes. Our general-purpose gain blocks are internally matched and offer various gain and linearity levels in a variety of package types, giving customers many options for system design.

    Key Features

    • Complete portfolio with a wide variety of parts
    • High levels of OIP3
    • Wide bandwidth
    • Ease of use for designers with internally matched parts

    Typical Applications

    • Base station
    • Cable TV
    • Defense communications
    • Optical
    • Point-to-point radio
    • Test and measurement equipment
